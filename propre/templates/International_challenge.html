About the Event

A Global Engineering Summit

The ENISE International Challenge is a multidisciplinary competition open to students from across the globe. For one week, teams will collaborate to solve a real-world engineering problem, focusing on sustainable development and social impact.


Our Goals

We aim to foster intercultural collaboration, promote a global perspective on engineering, and challenge the next generation of innovators to create solutions with a positive impact. It's an opportunity to network, learn, and compete on a global stage. (Head of Project: Clément FRENE)
